How can we help?

How do I book a ride?

Simply log in, enter your pickup and drop locations on the dashboard, and click "Confirm Booking". A verified driver will be assigned to you instantly.

How does the Student Discount work?

If you use a university email (like .edu or .ac.in), click the "Verify ID" button on the fare card to get an instant 25% discount.

Are the drivers verified?

Yes. All EasyRide drivers pass a rigorous background check and identity verification process to ensure passenger safety.
